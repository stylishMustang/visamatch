# VisaMatch – Smart Job Discovery Based on Visa Sponsorship

**VisaMatch** is a data-driven job search platform that helps international candidates find jobs that sponsor their specific visa type. Using real-time U.S. government data, VisaMatch connects users to employers actively sponsoring visas like H-1B, O-1, TN, and more.

## 🚀 What It Does

- ✅ **Matches jobs based on your visa type** (e.g., H-1B, OPT, CPT, O-1, TN)
- 📊 **Uses government labor data** (like PERM and LCA filings) to surface verified sponsors
- 🧠 **Intelligently ranks employers** based on historical approval rates, job categories, and locations
- 🔍 **Custom filters** for job title, company, city, and salary range
- 📬 **Personalized alerts** when new sponsorship-friendly jobs are posted

## 🔗 Data Sources

VisaMatch aggregates and parses public datasets from:
- 🇺🇸 **Department of Labor (DoL)** – LCA & PERM disclosures
- 🏛 **USCIS** – Petition approval stats
- 📄 **SEVIS** – F-1/OPT reporting data (optional)
- 🧩 Job boards + employer career pages

## 👥 Who It's For

- F-1 students on **OPT/CPT** seeking H-1B sponsorship
- Professionals on **H-1B**, **O-1**, or **TN** visas looking to switch jobs
- International grads navigating **green card pathways**
- Career advisors, recruiters, and immigration law firms

## 🌐 Sample Use Case

> Malak is on OPT and needs to find an H-1B-sponsoring role in tech. She logs into VisaMatch, selects "H-1B", sets her preferred city to San Francisco, and sees a curated list of software engineering jobs at companies that have filed H-1Bs in the past 3 years — ranked by approval rate and salary.
